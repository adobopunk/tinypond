Tiny Pond is a design-focused, digital co-op.

Tiny Pond began when three friends decided to forge their own path and be creative, innovative, and productive on their own terms. The three of us - Cory Sobon, Tifa Jade, and Mateo Julia-Wise, worked together for nearly three years, producing and designing some of the largest online esports events.

The three of us have worked directly with brands like Mountain Dew, Intel, Porsche, Verizon, and games publishers like Epic Games, Xbox, and Activision, to produce top-tier events for Fortnite, Sea of Thieves, Halo, Call of Duty, Apex Legends, and more. And we enjoyed doing it!

That's why we decided to take our talents as designers, animators, and event coordinators to the next level, and create our own studio.

As a co-op, we don't have a CEO or managers. We're a small team supporting one another. We're focused on doing great work, rather than answering to shareholders. That means every dollar you invest in Tiny Pond directly supports our cause: making great stuff, for you!

We couldn't be more excited about the launch of Tiny Pond, and we can't wait to work with you.

Cory Sobon Chief Outdoorsman
