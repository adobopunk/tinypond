Tiny Pond is a creative team that’s spent years working side-by-side and refining our craft, building not only our skillsets, but a deep mutual respect. Collaboration is at the heart of everything we do. We've worked together on projects for giants like Activision, Mountain Dew, Fortnite, Apex Legends, and Xbox, tag-teaming graphics and animation to deliver exceptional live experiences with professionalism, care, and a shared passion for doing great work.

The goal of Tiny Pond is simple: deliver great results, together. When you partner with us, you’re directly supporting a passionate team of independent artists, dedicated to creating exceptional tailor-made work that sets you up for success.

Tiny Pond may be a new venture, but our team's experience is vast, and the potential we bring is limitless. Let’s work together and make something amazing. We can't wait to bring your ideas to life.

Our Values

We believe that the best results come from open communication and shared problem-solving. Through our collaborative approach, we harness our collective strengths and expertise to create impactful results.

Embracing underserved perspectives enriches our work and deepens its impact. Our team is committed to partnering with and supporting causes that make a positive difference. On this team, we foster an environment where everyone feels valued, respected, and empowered.

We take pride in our leaps — and we’re always ready to explain our thinking process. Accountability means standing by our work and being transparent about our decisions, fostering trust and integrity in everything we do.

We believe the best results come from expertise and experience. We craft our art with human hands, not AI. We invest in our clients as people, not business partners. We're most successful when we're celebrating human connections and nurturing healthy relationships.

Our goal is to keep things clear and open. Trust goes both ways! We maintain transparency throughout our collaboration, and ask that you grant us the trust we need to make creative decisions you can rely on. Together, we can navigate the waters with clarity and purpose.

We’re dedicated to growth and change, ensuring we remain fresh, innovative, and ready to tackle any challenge. We're committed to innovation, we embrace research and development, and we'll never back down from a worthy challenge.

We don't want you to just dip your toes in the pond. Our aim is to build long-lasting relationships that grow over time. Together, we’ll develop a valuable partnership based on trust and open, friendly collaboration.

Our team believes in making the world better for everyone. We value sustainable practices and positive social impact initiatives, understanding that a thriving pond benefits all. Together, we will work to create a more sustainable and vibrant future.

Our goal is to deliver work that exceeds expectations. When you collaborate with our team, expect us to become your go-to creative solution, naturally adapting to meet your needs and bringing all your visions to life.
