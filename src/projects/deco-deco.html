Context

Deco Deco is a Los Angeles-based arts & crafts studio specializing in Decoden, the Japanese art form that transforms everyday accessories into highly-customized personal treasures.

Our challenge was to create a visual identity from the ground up. We knew we wanted to Deco Deco’s playful and welcoming spirit, while appealing to a creative and charm-loving target audience.

What We Did

We developed a complete brand identity for Deco Deco, where every element was designed to capture Deco Deco’s happy, cutesy vibe and create a cohesive, magical experience for their customers.

A custom logo featuring a soft script font for a handcrafted, personalized feel.

A pastel color palette (light pink, peach, and cream) to evoke warmth and charm.

Hand-illustrated icons (hearts, flowers, and ribbons) and retro-inspired patterns for a whimsical, detail-oriented aesthetic.

Design Process

Our process began with understanding and aligning our creative direction with Deco Deco’s essence. The brand needed to feel playful, feminine, and deeply creative.

We explored mood boards, color palettes, and typography options to establish a direction that felt both whimsical and grounded. We helped Deco Deco apply the identity across their web design, storefront, and social media posts.

The result is a brand identity that feels as unique and delightful as Deco Deco’s creations through a balanced blend of retro charm and modern appeal.

Brand Extensions

With a full set of brand guidelines, and a clean, scalable design that adapts to any platform, we positioned Deco Deco's internal teams to imagine new possibilities for extending the brand, enabling Deco Deco to work seamlessly across additional branded web platforms, merchandise, and content.

We developed custom T-shirt designs featuring Deco Deco's logo, extending the brand into wearable, shareable items.

Our design adapted cleanly to the brand's Shopify store, capturing Deco Deco’s aesthetic for web visitors.

A clean, recognizable look keeps Deco Deco content visible and easily identifyable on social media.
