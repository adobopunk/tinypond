The team at Deco Deco approached Tiny Pond with a full service design proposal: for the grand opening of their new LA-based decoden space, they would need a full branding package, and a website that would both share key information with customers and capture their playful pastel aesthetic.

We embraced the challenge and brought their vision to life, handling both branding and web design concurrently, and collaborating closely with the team to tailor every detail to their needs.

  • Brand Identity
  • Web Design

Context

Esports tournaments are rapidly evolving as a mainstream form of entertainment, but, events often lack cohesive branding and strategy to fully leverage the potential for audience engagement, sponsor alignment, and community-building.

Challenge

Create a memorable brand identity that resonates within the esports community, anchoring large-scale events with substantial prize pools and drawing popular influencers to compete, ensuring strong audience appeal.

Strategy

We collaborated with partners to develop unique branding for each event, ensuring each tournament aligned with overarching campaign and business goals while delivering consistently positive experiences for players and viewers alike.
