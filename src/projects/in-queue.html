Context

The esports industry is at a crossroads. With corporate interests and profit-driven models dominating the tournament landscape, the soul of competition often takes a backseat.

We founded In Queue to change that. Born from the collective passion of esports veterans, In Queue is an independent, worker-owned production and operations agency dedicated to authentic, community-driven competition.

To stand out in a crowded space, we needed a brand identity as bold and uncompromising as our vision.

Design Process

Most esports branding leans into aggressive, over-the-top aesthetics. In Queue needed something clean, confident, and iconic that would feel at home on a indie LAN stream or a championship trophy. We explored a wide range of directions before landing on a logo that felt iconic, ownable, and natural to animate.

The final mark features four outward-facing arrows, symbolizing our core values: Integrity, Community, Opportunity, and Excellence. Its emblem-style silhouette fits naturally into the esports space while echoing the feeling of a game queue animation, a subtle nod built into the brandâ€™s DNA.

Putting Values Front and Center

We knew that we wanted to launch In Queue with the promise that we're fighting to make competitive gaming more fun, more accessible, and uncompromised. Our announcement was accompanied by a brand video we built to gradually reveal the makeup of our logo, one element at a time, while also loudly announcing what we believe in, and why.
