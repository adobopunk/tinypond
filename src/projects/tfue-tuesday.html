Tfue Returns

A comeback for the ages.

After an extended hiatus from the internet, Tfue marked his triumphant return by bringing the iconic Tfue Tuesday series back to life, and our team got straight to work. By transforming footage from Tfue’s viral break announcement into a cinematic, comic-inspired journey, we kicked off the new Tfue Tuesday with a heroic narrative to position Tfue as Fortnite’s returning champion.
