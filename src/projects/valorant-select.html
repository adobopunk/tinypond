Valorant Select redefined the tournament viewing experience by integrating live character bans and selections into Riot Games' official Valorant Champions Tour. Tiny Pond's art team crafted a custom broadcast solution that seamlessly displayed these critical moments in real-time, heightening the intensity of the competition.

Context

As part of Riot Games' official Valorant Champions Tour, Valorant Select introduced a unique tournament format with character bans and selections as a central gameplay element.

Strategy

Develop a unique broadcast experience capable of bringing the character selection and ban process to life in real-time, making it a focal point of the broadcast.

Solution

Building custom screens and utilizing the game's API, we allowed viewers to witness the live, interactive dynamics of character picks and bans, adding a new layer of excitement to the Valorant tournament.
