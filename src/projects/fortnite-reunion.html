Tiny Pond's artists have proudly collaborated with Epic Games over the years. For Fortnite's return to the original Battle Royale map, we designed the event branding and announcement trailer for a $100,000 throwback tournament that saw OG Fortnite streamers battle it out, class reunion style.

Context

Epic Games was returning the original battle royale map to Fortnite, in a limited-time event called Fortnite OG, whisking the game back to its Chapter 1 era.

Strategy

Celebrate what original fans loved about Fortnite OG with a class-reunion style event, inviting OG influencers and fans alike to a nostalgia-fueled throwdown.

Solution

An event that vibrantly captures the charm of the OG map, highlighting fan-favorite locations, and embracing the global impact of the battle royale giant.

Announcement Trailer

What's a reunion without a little cake?

To pull off a show-stopping announcement, we worked closely with the team at Epic Games to learn exactly which locations would return and featured them on a charming 3D cake. A system of over 30,000 particles brought our delicious design to life, with frosting drop trees and vibrant sprinkles rounding out the edible terrain.
