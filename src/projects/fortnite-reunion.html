A blend of cinematic flair and gritty intensity for a battle royale showcase like no other.

Tiny Pond's artists have had the exciting opportunity to collaborate with Epic Games throughout the years. When Fortnite brought back the original Battle Royale map, our team got to design a throwback tournament with $100,000 in prizing that saw many of the biggest Fortnite streamers come together to celebrate the return of the OG map.

Context

Epic Games was returning the original battle royale map to Fortnite, in a limited-time event called Fortnite OG, whisking the game back to its Chapter 1 era.

Strategy

Celebrate what original fans loved about Fortnite OG with a class-reunion style event, inviting OG influencers and fans alike to a nostalgia-fueled tournament.

Solution

An event that feels vibrantly celebratory, encapsulating the charm of the OG map by highlighting its returning locations, and bringing fans around the globe
