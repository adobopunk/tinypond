UltraCaius hired Tiny Pond to build a new suite of visuals to coincide with the evolution of his VTuber model and a new era of streaming for the Kitsune of Eternal Blossom.

We were delighted to collaborate on this project, which included social graphics, a cinematic animation, and a new set of streaming animations built from the ground up for the newly transformed UltraCaius.

We built a complete immersive streaming graphics package designed to transport UltraCaius's Twitch audience to a magical realm informed by the themes, styles, and character that make his community incredible. Celestial Scene

UltraCaius's affinity for all things space resonated with us, and building a Makoto Shinkai-influenced night sky environment was a natural approach for UltraCaius's intermission cinematics. Built in Blender with heavy use of shader magic and geometry nodes, Tiny Pond built this cherry blossom scene with auroras, a comet, and rippling grass all themed around UltraCaius's color scheme and character themes.
