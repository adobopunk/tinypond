Context

Baehino is a Twitch personality and VTuber who describes herself as "a demon overlord from the Demon Association." With her unique blend of cute and evil aesthetics, Tiny Pond aimed to create a stream package that captured her demonic charm with dynamic visuals that would captivate viewers for hours on end.

What We Did

We designed a full suite of broadcast visuals and brand assets to elevate Baehino’s streaming experience. Every element was crafted to reflect Baehino’s persona as a demon overlord, creating a cohesive and immersive experience for her viewers.

Looping, high-quality scenes optimized for 4+ hour streams, complete with a spooky scene transition, featuring hand-drawn animated elements to add dynamism and personality.

Custom typography and a complete branding overhaul to define her unique visual language that blends cute and evil, in a flexible system that makes it easy to customize and create new scenes on the fly.

Updated profile page elements, stream banners, and social media assets to unify Baehino’s brand across platforms.

Design Process

Baehino’s unique mix of cute and evil aesthetics informed every design choice, resulting in a striking color palette that was both inviting and mysterious. We leaned into fonts that fused sharp, angular lines with rounded edges—perfectly embodying Baehino’s "demon overlord" persona and her playful yet formidable character.

We carried this vision throughout her Twitch profile and social media platforms, refreshing her profile panels with cohesive illustrations and icons. Eye-catching stream banners and social media graphics were all hand-crafted to reflect her brand’s personality, creating a seamless experience for her audience whether watching her streams or engaging with her content online.
