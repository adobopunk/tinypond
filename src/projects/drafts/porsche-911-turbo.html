When Rocket League debuted their collaboration with Porsche, Tiny Pond's art team got to team up with the automotive legend to design a $50,000 tournament showcasing the new 911 Turbo, and hyping up the big collaboration.

Context

Rocket League's Season 12 highlights a corrupted, hack-themed cyberpunk world alongside a collaboration with Porsche that debuts the 911 Turbo body.

Strategy

Shine the spotlight on the 911 Turbo with a tournament where the biggest names in Rocket League showcase the Porsche 911 Turbo for fans around the world.

Solution

We collaborated with Rocket League's art team to bring Porsche a vibrant, animated broadcast with real-time stats and the season's signature cyberpunk art style.

Design

Is that a glitch in the Matrix?

Taking direction from Rocket League's Season 12 branding, a looping visual glitch serves as the anchoring visual, while an iconic event logo tells a story in itself, with letters appearing to gain speed as they move in weight from left to right.

Broadcast Design

A high-energy event calls for an equally high energy broadcast.

Each screen was carefully crafted to maintain the sleek yet edgy vibe that fit both Porsche’s brand and Rocket League’s audience. A custom overlay, integrated with the Rocket League API, allowed for real-time updates and live stats, blending dynamic visuals with crucial game information to keep fans fully engaged in the action.
