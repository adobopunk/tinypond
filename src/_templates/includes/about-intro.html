What is Tiny Pond?

Tiny Pond is a creative studio and consultancy created by Tifa Jade and Mateo Julia-Wise. We worked side-by-side at an agency for over three years, collaborating on designs for gaming brands like Mountain Dew, Fortnite, and Xbox.

Now, we bring our expertise directly to you as Tiny Pond.

Our goal is simple: deliver great results, together. When you partner with us, you’re directly supporting independent artists, dedicated to creating exceptional bespoke design systems and assets that set you and your team up for success.

Tiny Pond is a new venture for us, but our experience is vast, and the potential we bring is limitless. Let’s work together and make something amazing. We can't wait to bring your ideas to life.
