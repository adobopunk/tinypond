What is Tiny Pond?

Tiny Pond is a creative duo created by Tifa Jade and Mateo Julia-Wise. We've spent years working side by side and refining our craft, while building a deep mutual respect.

Together, we've collaborated on designs and animations for giants like Activision, Mountain Dew, Fortnite, Apex Legends, and Xbox, tag-teaming graphics and animation to deliver exceptional experiences with professionalism, care, and a shared passion for doing great work.

The goal of Tiny Pond is simple: deliver great results, together. When you partner with us, you’re directly supporting independent artists, dedicated to creating exceptional bespoke work that sets you and your team up for success.

Tiny Pond may be a new venture, but our experience is vast, and the potential we bring is limitless. Let’s work together and make something amazing. We can't wait to bring your ideas to life.
