What is Tiny Pond?

Tiny Pond is a creative studio and consultancy created by Tifa Jade and Mateo Julia-Wise. We worked side-by-side at BoomTV for over three years, collaborating on designs for gaming brands like Mountain Dew, Fortnite, and Xbox.

Now, we're excited to bring the same professionalism, care, and shared passion for design and animation that we brought to the biggest names in gaming, straight to you.

The goal of Tiny Pond is simple: deliver great results, together. When you partner with us, you’re directly supporting independent artists, dedicated to creating exceptional bespoke design systems and assets that set you and your team up for success.

Tiny Pond is a new venture for us, but our experience is vast, and the potential we bring is limitless. Let’s work together and make something amazing. We can't wait to bring your ideas to life.
