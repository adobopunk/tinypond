Tiny Pond is A Dedicated Digital Design Co-Op.

Tiny Pond was born from a shared vision among three friends — Cory Sobon, Tifa Jade, and Mateo Julia-Wise. Having collaborated for nearly three years at BoomTV, we specialized in crafting high-profile esports events and working with leading gaming influencers in both pro and semi-pro competitive circuits.

Our collective efforts earned us the trust of major brands like Mountain Dew, Intel, Porsche, and Verizon, as well as renowned game publishers such as Epic Games, Xbox, and Activision. We've designed and produced memorable events for titles like Fortnite, Sea of Thieves, Halo, Call of Duty, and Apex Legends. From dynamic video announcements to striking broadcast graphics and seamless event management, our efforts and our craftsmanship consistently exceeded expectations.

We knew that in order to further our creativity, innovation, and dedication, we'd need to take it on a new venture. Now, we're excited to bring that expertise to Tiny Pond and offer it to you directly.

At Tiny Pond, we operate as a cooperative, focused on collaboration and mutual support. Everyone has an equal say in our operations, and we're each accountable for our own work. Without a traditional hierarchy of managers, or a CEO with shareholders to answer to, our goal is simply to produce great results. That means every dollar you invest in Tiny Pond directly supports our cause: making more awesome stuff, for you!

Let’s explore how Tiny Pond can bring your ideas to life. We can't wait to work with you.

Our Values

At Tiny Pond, our values guide every aspect of our work. These are our guiding stars - the principles that drive our passion and commitment to both our clients and our team. We’re not just about making ripples—we’re here to create waves of excellence.

Embrace Collaboration. We believe that teamwork makes the dream work. Like frogs in a pond, we know that the best results come when we all leap together. Collaboration is our secret ingredient to keeping our ecosystem healthy.

Own Your Work. Just like a frog’s leap, we take pride in our jumps — and we’re always ready to explain how we made it. Accountability means standing by our work and being transparent about our process. No need for lily pads to hide behind!

Happy People, Happy Frogs. We’re all about people, not just pond life. Our strategies are designed to keep our clients and team at the center of our universe, because happy frogs and people make the best teams.

Think Global, Act Local. With team members across the US, we use technology to keep us all on the same lily pad, making sure distance doesn’t slow us down. We embrace collaboration with groups of all scales and sizes, and we never forget our roots.

Trust in the Pond. Trust is as important to us as a frog’s first hop. We promise to keep things clear and open — no murky waters here. That being said, if we see something that needs a little ribbit of improvement, we'll tell you. Trust goes both ways in any relationship, including ours!

Continuous Evolution. Just like a tadpole transforms into a frog, we’re all about growth and change. We embrace every opportunity to evolve, learn, and improve, ensuring we stay fresh, innovative, and ready to take on any challenge.

Cultivate Long-Term Partnerships. We’re not into fleeting frog jumps. Our aim is to build long-lasting relationships that grow over time, just like a pond’s ecosystem. Together, we’ll develop a deep understanding and make waves that last.

Do Good, Be Good. We aim to prioritize relationships that share our commitment to making the world a better place, through sustainable practices and social impact initiatives. We're particular about the work we take on, because a thriving pond benefits every frog, fish, and friend within it.

Excellence in Everything We Do. We’re dedicated to hopping above and beyond. Our goal is to deliver work that exceeds expectations, reaching for the stars while keeping our feet firmly on the lily pads. At Tiny Pond, we’re all about making a splash.
